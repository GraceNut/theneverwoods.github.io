One Caring Adult

"Every Kid Is ONE Caring Adult Away from Being a Success Story."

One Caring Adult is an online curriculum and community that reaches out to adults to have a positive, lasting affect on kids. The brand had to be approachable and fun but still communicate authority. The site is a mix marketing pages to convert users as well as an enrollment flow, app pages, and a growing list of content/lesson pages.
